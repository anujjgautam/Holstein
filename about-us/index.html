About Us

- Holstein Nutrition

Who are we?

Holstein Nutrients is best known as a livestock feed-producing company. It is a sister concern of MMC Group founded in 2001 under the name of Mahajan Molasses Company. Being a trusted and proud supplier of top-quality raw materials for cattle feed, we started our subsidiary to produce the best quality cattle feed under the brand HOLSTEIN.

As a subsidiary of MMC Group, Holstein benefits from the strength and expertise of our parent company. With MMC's vast experience in processing and supplying key raw materials like molasses, maize, and DORB, we have access to the finest ingredients for balanced cattle feed. This connection allows us to focus on producing the best quality feed and ensure superior nutrition and health for livestock. MMC's strong reputation and resources enable us to consistently deliver high-standard cattle feed products. Together, we ensure that farmers receive only the best feed for their cattle.

Our Mission

At Holstein, we improve cattle health and productivity by producing feed that is rich in nutrients. We believe that healthy cattle are the foundation of a successful farm, and we are committed to producing nutritious and best-quality feed. We use carefully selected ingredients that are far superior and procured ethically. From this, we formulate balanced and nutritious feed and aim to enhance livestock well-being and performance. Our focus is providing farmers with reliable and effective feed solutions to promote growth, boost immunity, and contribute to the overall health of cattles.

Our Vision

Our vision at Holstein is to lead the way in producing the highest quality cattle feed by sourcing the best raw materials and utilizing advanced processing techniques. How do we do this? By leveraging cutting-edge technology and the strengths of our parent company, MMC Group in procuring key ingredients like molasses for cattle feed, maize, and DORB, we have the advantage of producing cattle feed with top-grade materials. We prioritize R&D in animal nutrition by working with world-renowned experts to develop high-quality nutritious feed for livestock.

What Sets Us Apart?

Best Raw Materials

Regularly monitoring the feed quality and adjusting based on the cattle's age, weight, and purpose can make a big difference. By investing in the right feed mix, you're not only supporting their growth but also ensuring they stay healthy and productive, and yield the best results for the milk production. With some extra stuff like probiotics, it can help cows digest their food better, making them healthier. It's a good idea to change up the feed now and then so they don't get bored and stop eating as much. Getting advice from a feed expert can help make sure your cows are getting just the right mix of food. Healthy and happy cows mean better milk production!

State-of-the-art Equipment

Our equipment goes beyond just mixing and weighing. We aim to keep the livestock healthy and happy. Our monitoring tools track their health and growth. By investing in this technology, we're not only boosting productivity but also taking care of our environment. Efficient feed usage means less waste, which is better for the planet. Our goal is to create a sustainable farming system that benefits both the animals and the farmers. When livestock are well-fed and cared for, they thrive. It leads to higher-quality milk production. This approach helps us meet the growing demand for healthy food while supporting the well-being of our animals and the earth.

Hygenic Warehousing

We understand that keeping feed clean is essential for the health of your livestock. Our team conducts regular checks to make sure everything is in top shape. We also store different types of feed separately to avoid any mix-ups. With these practices, we can be sure that our products stay fresh and safe for cows to eat. Healthy feed means healthy cows, which ultimately leads to better milk and meat production. Our commitment to hygiene doesn't just protect our products but also shows our dedication to providing the best for the animals.
