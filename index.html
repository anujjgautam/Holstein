Trusted & Tested By Farmers

Holstein Nutrients is best known as a livestock feed-producing company. It is a sister concern of MMC Group founded in 2001 under the name of Mahajan Molasses Company. Being a trusted and proud supplier of top-quality raw materials for cattle feed, we started our subsidiary to produce the best quality cattle feed under the brand HOLSTEIN.

As a subsidiary of MMC Group, Holstein benefits from the strength and expertise of our parent company. With MMC's vast experience in processing and supplying key raw materials like molasses, maize, and DORB, we have access to the finest ingredients for balanced cattle feed. This connection allows us to focus on producing the best quality feed and ensure superior nutrition and health for livestock. MMC’s strong reputation and resources enable us to consistently deliver high-standard cattle feed products. Together, we ensure that farmers receive only the best feed for their cattle.

Our Mission

At Holstein, our mission is to improve cattle health and productivity by producing feed that is rich in high-quality nutrients. We believe that healthy cattle are the foundation of a successful farm, and we are committed to producing highly nutritious and best-quality feed for livestock. We use carefully selected ingredients that are nutritionally far superior and procured ethically from selected regions famous for producing high-quality crops. From this, we formulate balanced and nutritious feed and aim to enhance livestock well-being and performance. Our focus is on providing farmers with reliable and effective feed solutions that promote growth, boost immunity, and contribute to the overall health of their herds. Holstein is committed to producing quality feed & zero tolerance for compromising animal health.

Best Raw Materials

The best raw materials for cow cattle feed include grains like corn and barley for energy, and soybean meal and alfalfa for protein. Fiber-rich ingredients such as hay and silage support digestion. Adding vitamins and minerals ensures a balanced diet, promoting overall health and productivity.
