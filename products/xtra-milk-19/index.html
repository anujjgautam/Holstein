HOLSTEIN XTRA MILK-19

(Specialized Feed for Moderate-Yielding Cows in Mid-Lactation)
Holstein Xtra Milk-19

HOLSTEIN XTRA MILK-19 provides complete nutrition to support mid-lactating cows, ensuring they maintain health and productivity.

Quick Info:

Net Weight: 50 Kg

Category: Cattle Feed

Tag: Xtra Milk-19

Key Features Feeding Instructions

HOLSTEIN XTRA MILK-19 is tailored to meet the nutritional demands of cows during mid-lactation, ensuring they receive balanced nutrition that supports their health, milk production, and overall performance. This feed is essential for maintaining sustained productivity and health in moderate-yielding cows.

Balanced Nutrition:
Specifically formulated for cows yielding 10-19 kg of milk per day during mid-lactation.

Improved Milk Production:
Enhances both the quality (fat content) and quantity of milk produced.

Maintains Health Condition and Optimal BCS:
Supports overall health and ensures cows maintain an ideal Body Condition Score (BCS).

Highly Digestible:
Enriched with easily digestible sources of protein and energy, crucial for sustained milk production.

Reduces Rumen Stress:
Formulated to reduce stress on the rumen, which can enhance overall productivity.

Fortified with Trace Minerals and Vitamins:
Includes essential nutrients to improve reproductive ability and boost immunity in cows.

  • Feed as Total Mixed Ration (TMR):
    Combine with green fodder, silage, straw, and other feed components to create a balanced diet.
  • Dry Feeding:
    Ensure the feed is provided dry; avoid soaking. If soaking is necessary, limit to 15 minutes to maintain feed efficacy.
