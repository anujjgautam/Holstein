HOLSTEIN BUFF CARE-2800

(Specialized Feed for Buffaloes Yielding up to 10 kg Milk per Day)
Holstein Buff Care-2800

HOLSTEIN BUFF CARE-2800 is a compounded cattle feed specifically designed for buffaloes that yield up to 10 kg of milk per day. It is formulated with essential trace minerals, vitamins, and nutrients to provide complete nutrition, support health, mitigate production stress, and enhance milk production and fat content.

Quick Info:

Net Weight: 50 Kg

Category: Cattle Feed

Tag: Buff Care-2800

Key Features Feeding Instructions

HOLSTEIN BUFF CARE-2800 is aimed at providing comprehensive nutrition to mid-lactating buffaloes, supporting their health, productivity, and milk quality while ensuring they receive balanced and effective nutrition through a properly managed feeding regimen.

Balanced Nutrition:
Tailored for mid-lactating buffaloes (up to 10 kg of milk per day).

Improved Milk Production:
Enhances both the quality and quantity of milk produced.

Maintains Optimal Health and Body Condition Score (BCS):
Supports overall health and ensures buffaloes maintain an ideal BCS.

Fortified with Trace Minerals and Vitamins:
Boosts reproductive ability and strengthens the immune system of buffaloes.

  • Feed as Total Mixed Ration (TMR):
    Combine with green fodder, silage, straw, and other feed components to create a balanced diet.
  • Dry Feeding:
    Ensure the feed is given dry. If soaking is necessary, limit soaking time to no more than 15 minutes to maintain feed efficacy.
